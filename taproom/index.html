Wingman Brewers Taproom

Our taproom and brew house are open Tuesday through Sunday. Come down and sample our beers, enjoy a pint, and go home with a growler. We always have our own beer on tap, and do our best to include a guest beer or two along with a craft hard cider for your friends who aren't so fond of barley.

Though we don't serve food, you're more than welcome to bring your own. You will often find a local food truck parked out front, and there are a variety of great restaurants nearby which offer take-out and delivery options.

  • Monday through Thursday 2–9pm
  • Friday and Saturday 2–11pm
  • Sunday 2–9pm
